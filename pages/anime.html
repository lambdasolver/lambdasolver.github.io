So you're interested in my taste in anime. Since 2020, I have been an anime fan. I have seen over 150 anime so far.

I enjoy watching various genres. I realised that genres I had previously detested in English films were actually quite interesting in Anime. So yes, I generally like all genres. The genres I found to be the most fascinating among them include horror, thrillers, and slice of life. Yes, you heard correctly; I also enjoy the horror genre.

Before I started watching anime, I used to watch a lot of English films. There, the horror was merely a jump scare. It's not horror, though. What I really want is psychological horror, the kind of terror that will shock you the most. That fit in with certain horror anime really well.

I also read manga. I read manga whenever I start an anime that is presently running and can't wait for the next season. I haven't read a lot of manga. barely even crosses the number 10.

My Favourite Anime Character

My favourite anime character overall is Light Yagami from Death Note. Also to be precise I like the character Light Yagami portraited in the english dub version of Death Note

Hear me out before you start to criticise me. First of all, if you have watched Death Note, you should be aware that I only take the Death Note up until L's death into consideration. After that it becomes bogus and it feels like Near is using GTA cheatcodes. Additionally, upon L's passing, Light develops a maniacal sense of self-worth. He wasn't the smart, perceptive youngster he was when L was alive.

Here's why I like Light:

  • He is really brilliant, to start. From Isagi Yoichi from Blue Lock, Shikamaru from Naruto, Senku from Dr. Stone, to Ayanokoji from Classroom of the Elite, I enjoy all intellectual anime characters. He is also not a prodigy talent, in contrast to all the other prodigy personalities. He is intelligent with his hardworking. And respect those individuals who, while having little natural skill, have worked incredibly hard to become experts in their subjects. I like Light in part because of this. And this motivates me very much. Since i am always surrounded by very intelligent people in my academic life, this characterist of Light Yagami makes me stay motivated towards my goal of being a Computer Scientist.
  • The second point is highly particular to the dub version. The voice actor did such an amazing job, the way he behaved and speech constantly give the image of someone highly intelligent. You probably already know that when you speak to certain really bright people, their behaviour radiates intelligence, instantly elevating him to a position of great respect. Actually, given his age and character description, I believe the English voice fits him well. He demonstrates his intelligence by the way he thinks and interacts with others.
  • You might now despise me. But I kind of concur with his ideology, the way in which he began doing everything. But once more, 'I kind of agree'
  • And i am aware that many people would accuse him of killing innocent bystanders and manipulating Misa's emotions. However, you already know that what Light is doing is illegal. Then, what is the best approach to dispel the suspicion that someone is watching him to find out if he is the murderer or not? It is to show them that assassination still occurs even when he does nothing. Light is currently limited to acting alone. Ryuk is only an observer, thus he cannot ask him to do it. Therefore, it stands to reason that any criminal who makes the news must be killed, even if they do not pose a serious threat to society.

    And coming to the Misa's emotion. Dude, Light is engaging in very illegal activities. If he is apprehended, his family will also experience a life suffering in addition to him receiving a death sentence.
